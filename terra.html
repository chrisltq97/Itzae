Sample three-day rhythm

Here’s how we pace days across rainforest ridges and volcanic valleys. Every hike is tailored to your energy, curiosity, and sense of wonder.

  1. Day 1

    Meet your naturalist guide for a sunrise ridge hike, coffee at a mountain finca, and a golden overlook above the Gulf of Nicoya.

  2. Day 2

    Descend to a secluded waterfall for swimming, a chef-packed picnic, and optional canyoning with expert safety teams.

  3. Day 3

    Traverse cloud forest trails to volcanic hot springs before a starlit tasting menu featuring local produce and cacao.
